InMoov Physical to Digital Mapping

1. Camera Ratio (Raw Input) 0.40

This represents the Tip-to-Wrist / Base-to-Wrist ratio detected by MediaPipe. 0.4 is your calibrated "Closed" and 3.0 is your "Open".

2. Normalized Value (0.0 to 1.0) 0.00

Normalization makes your hand size irrelevant. Whether you have big or small hands, 0.0 is always closed and 1.0 is always open.

3. Servo Angle (20° to 160°) 20.0°

This is the final signal sent to the Arduino. It scales the 0-1 percentage to the physical mechanical limits of your InMoov servos.
