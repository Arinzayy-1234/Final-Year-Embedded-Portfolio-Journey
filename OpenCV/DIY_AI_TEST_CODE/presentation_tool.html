InMoov Hand: The Logic Pipeline

Step 1: Physical Ratio (Hand Tracking)
Camera Measurement (Tip-to-Wrist) 0.40

Your unique hand gives a raw ratio between 0.4 (Closed) and 3.0 (Open). This changes depending on the person.

Step 2: Normalization (The Common Language)
Universal Scale (0.0 to 1.0) 0.00

We convert your personal numbers into a universal percentage. 0% is always closed, 100% is always open, regardless of hand size.

Step 3: Servo Mapping (Robot Motion)
Servo Signal (Degrees) 20.0°

Finally, we map that percentage to your InMoov servos (20° to 160°). This is what the Arduino receives.
