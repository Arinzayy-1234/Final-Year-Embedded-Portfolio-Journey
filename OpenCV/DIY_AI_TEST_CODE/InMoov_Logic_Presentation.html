InMoov Logic Presentation Tool

1 Raw Camera Ratio (Tip-to-Wrist / Palm) 0.40

This is the direct measurement from MediaPipe. It changes based on how much you curl your finger. Your physical limits are 0.4 (closed) and 3.0 (open).

2 Normalized Value (Universal Scale) 0.00

We subtract the closed limit and divide by the range. This creates a "Universal Language" of 0.0 to 1.0. This works for any hand size!

3 Physical Servo Angle 20.0°

Finally, we map the 0.0-1.0 scale to your servo's physical travel (20° to 160°). This is what actually moves the InMoov arm.
